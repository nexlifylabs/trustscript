## 🔒 Zero PII Collection — Privacy Isn't a Feature. It's the Foundation.
Most review platforms quietly collect more customer data than they ever need. TrustScript is built differently - from the ground up, with a strict **Zero PII policy**. We never collect, store, or share any personally identifiable information from your customers. Ever.

**What We Actually Touch (And Why)**

TrustScript only ever handles two things:
- **Order number** - used solely to verify the purchase and power your analytics. Nothing else.
- **One-way hashed email address** - an irreversible, anonymised token used only for opt-out tracking. It cannot be decoded, reversed, or traced back to the original email address under any circumstances.

That's it. No names. No addresses. No profiles. No tracking.

**Your Emails, Your Domain, Your Control.**
Review invitation emails are sent directly from **your own WordPress domain**, using your own SMTP server or a transactional email service you already trust. Your brand stays front and centre, deliverability stays high, and no email data passes through TrustScript's servers.

**Optional Context Fields — Your Call.**
Product name and order date are completely optional. We recommend enabling them - they help customers recall their purchase more vividly, which leads to richer, more detailed reviews. But you can toggle them off at any time without losing a single feature.

**The Result:**
Your customers remain completely anonymous. You get rich, verified, meaningful feedback. And your store stays fully compliant with **GDPR, CCPA**, and global privacy standards - by design, not by checkbox.

## ✅ Verifiable Reviews — Every Review is Provably Real

Trust is easy to claim. TrustScript lets you *prove* it.

Anyone can say their reviews are genuine. TrustScript lets your customers actually check for themselves.

Every review collected through TrustScript comes with a built-in verification system — so anyone who wants to confirm a review is real can do so in under 10 seconds, without needing to trust anyone's word for it.

**The Verified Purchase Badge**

Once a review passes verification, a green **✔ Verified Purchase** badge appears automatically next to the reviewer's name on your product page. It tells every future shopper that this feedback came from a real customer who genuinely bought and used your product — not a made-up account or a favour from a friend.

**The Verification Token — See Exactly What Happened**

Clicking the badge opens a small pop-up showing the review's unique **Verification Token** — a one-of-a-kind code that is permanently tied to that specific review. Think of it like a receipt for the review itself. From there, the visitor can copy the token and click **Verify on TrustScript**, which opens the public verification page at [nexlifylabs.com/verify-review](https://nexlifylabs.com/verify-review).

Paste the token, hit verify, and the full review record appears in seconds — showing:

- **Store & Product Info** — store name, product reviewed, and a masked purchase ID to keep it private
- **Review Timeline** — when the review was submitted, when it was approved, and the star rating given
- **Verification Checks** — whether the customer approved the review, whether the email was verified, and whether AI writing assistance was used
- **Security & Privacy Status** — confirms that no names, emails, or personal details are stored anywhere
- **Full Review Content** — the customer's original words exactly as they typed them, every AI-suggested version (if they used the writing assistant), which version the customer picked, and the final published review — nothing hidden, nothing changed behind the scenes

**Why This Matters**

When AI writing assistance is used, TrustScript is completely open about it — showing the original text, every suggestion offered, and exactly what the customer chose. The customer's voice is always preserved. This kind of openness is what separates a trustworthy review from one that just *looks* trustworthy.

Shoppers who check a review leave your store feeling confident. Businesses get solid proof that every review on their site is real, honest, and earned - with a clear record to back it up.

## ✨ AI Writing Assistant: Optional, Honest, and Always in the Customer's Control

Not everyone finds it easy to put their experience into words. TrustScript's built-in AI writing assistant is there for those moments — a gentle nudge that helps customers say what they already mean, just a little more clearly.

**Completely Optional: Always the Customer's Choice**

After typing their review, customers see two buttons: **Submit Review** to publish it exactly as written, or **Enhance** to get a little help from AI. There's no pressure, no default, no auto-correction happening in the background. If they're happy with what they wrote, they just hit submit and they're done.

**What Happens When They Choose Enhance**

TrustScript takes the customer's original words and offers **three thoughtfully rewritten versions** — each with a slightly different style, all staying true to what the customer actually said. The original version is always shown at the top so nothing gets lost.

Each variant shows the word count, and customers can:
- **Select any version** they prefer — including the original
- **Edit any variant** before submitting using the inline edit button
- **Try Again** to generate a fresh set of suggestions
- **Go back** to the review form and make changes from scratch

The AI fixes spelling, smooths out grammar, and improves flow — but it never adds details the customer didn't mention, never changes their meaning, and never invents an experience. What comes out is still 100% the customer's story, just easier to read.

**What This Means for Your Store**

Customers who struggle with writing still leave great reviews. Future shoppers get clearer, more readable feedback. And your analytics get richer, more consistent data to work with — all without compromising a single word of genuine experience.

And when AI is used? It's always disclosed — visible on the review card and fully transparent on the verification page. Nothing is hidden.

**Smart Enough to Handle Refunds and Cancellations**

Real businesses deal with refunds. TrustScript handles them cleanly so your review data stays accurate without any cleanup on your end.

The moment you mark an order as **refunded or cancelled in WooCommerce**, the corresponding review link updates instantly:

- **Full cancellation or refund** — the entire review link expires. The customer can no longer submit a review for that order.
- **Partial refund on a multi-product order** — only the affected products are removed from the form. The customer's link stays active, but when they open it, the cancelled items have quietly disappeared. No error message, no confusion — they simply see the products they still have.

This works in real time, automatically, for every order — no manual intervention, no chasing down expired links, no awkward reviews for products that were returned.

**The Result**

A self-managing review system that stays accurate, stays clean, and keeps running — whether you have 10 orders a month or 10,000. You get more reviews, better data, and zero extra work.

**Technical Details:**

*Data Sent to TrustScript Service:*
- Order number (used solely for purchase verification and analytics)
- One-way hashed email address (irreversible; used only for opt-out tracking)
- Webhook URL (where TrustScript posts the completed review back to your site)
- Source identifier (platform type, e.g. woocommerce)
- Rating, photo, and video collection flags (control what fields appear in the TrustScript review form)
- Product name and order date (optional; controlled by plugin settings)

- Product name, product ID, product SKU, and a per-product token (required for multi-product orders only)

Note: Customer review text, star rating, and media are NOT sent by the plugin. They are collected
directly by TrustScript's review form and returned to your site via webhook after the customer submits.

*Data Received from TrustScript — Step 1 (Initial API Response):*
- Unique security token (stored for tracking and duplicate prevention)
- Duplicate flag (indicates if this order was already processed)
- Customer opted-out flag (indicates if customer has unsubscribed)

*Data Received from TrustScript — Step 2 (Webhook, after customer submits review):*
- Final review text (customer-written, optionally AI-enhanced)
- Star rating (1–5)
- Verification token (for the public review verification page)
- Uploaded media URLs (photos and/or videos)
- Verification hash (for webhook authenticity validation)

*Security & Verification:*
- ✅ Unique token format validated (32+ alphanumeric characters)
- ✅ Webhook timestamp verified (must be within 60 minutes to prevent replay attacks)
- ✅ API key authentication via Bearer token with timing-safe comparison
- ✅ Domain validation ensures API key only works for the registered WordPress site
- ✅ Duplicate prevention: reviews cannot be published twice with the same token
- ✅ Verification tokens allow public review authenticity checks at nexlifylabs.com/verify-review
- ✅ All data transmitted securely over HTTPS only
- ✅ Reviews stored in WordPress comment system with full audit trail metadata
- ✅ Zero PII: no names, emails, or personal identifiers stored on TrustScript servers

== Installation ==

1. Upload the plugin files to `/wp-content/plugins/trustscript/`
2. Activate the plugin through the 'Plugins' menu in WordPress
3. Sign up for a free TrustScript account at https://nexlifylabs.com
4. Get your API key from https://nexlifylabs.com/dashboard/api-keys
5. Navigate to TrustScript → Settings in your WordPress admin
6. Enter your API key and test the connection
7. Configure your review settings in TrustScript → Review Settings
8. Start collecting reviews automatically

== Frequently Asked Questions ==

= Do I need a TrustScript account? =
Yes, this plugin connects your WooCommerce store to the TrustScript 
service. You can sign up for free at https://nexlifylabs.com and get 
25 review requests per month at no cost.

= Is the plugin free? =
Yes, the plugin itself is free and open source (GPL-licensed). 
TrustScript offers a free plan with 25 review requests per month, 
with paid plans for higher volume.

= Does this work without WooCommerce? =
No, TrustScript requires WooCommerce to be installed and active. It 
connects to WooCommerce orders to trigger review requests 
automatically.

= Can customers write their own reviews? =
Absolutely. Customers always write their own review first. The AI 
writing assistant is completely optional — it only offers suggestions 
if the customer chooses to use it, and their original words are always 
preserved and shown on the verification page.

= Are photo and video reviews supported? =
Yes. Customers can attach up to 5 photos or videos directly to their 
review. TrustScript automatically compresses images before upload — so 
even large photos taken on a phone or camera (which are often 5–10MB+) 
upload quickly without any errors or file size warnings.

Supported formats: JPG, PNG, and WebP images (compressed automatically); 
MP4 videos up to 100MB. All media is displayed in a lightbox gallery 
on your product page.

= Does TrustScript store personal customer data? =
No. TrustScript follows a strict Zero PII policy. The only data 
handled is the order number (for verification) and a one-way hashed 
email address (for opt-out tracking only). This hash cannot be 
reversed. No names, email addresses, or personal identifiers are 
stored on TrustScript servers.

= Is there a limit on how many reviews I can collect? =
The free plan includes 25 review requests per month. Paid monthly 
plans include a monthly quota for AI-enhanced reviews (250, 500, or 
2,000 depending on your plan).

If you subscribe to an annual plan, the AI enhancement quota still 
applies — but once you reach it, you can continue collecting unlimited 
standard reviews (without AI assistance) for the rest of that month. 
Your AI quota resets the following month.

View all plans at https://nexlifylabs.com/pricing

= Can I customize the review request email? =
Yes. Email templates are managed from your NexlifyLabs dashboard at 
nexlifylabs.com — not from inside WordPress. You can customize the 
following fields for each email:

- Email subject line
- Header label (small text above the title)
- Email title
- Greeting message
- Thank you message body
- Review request call-to-action text

Available variables:
{customer_name}, {store_name}, {product_name}, {product_image},
{order_number}, {order_date}, {order_total}

{opt_out_link} and {store_name} are inserted automatically — you do 
not need to add them manually.

Product image, product name, order number, order date, and order total 
are all pulled automatically from WooCommerce at send time. This works 
for both single-product and multi-product orders.

At the scheduled send time, TrustScript delivers the template to your 
WordPress site, which then fills in the placeholders with real order 
data and sends the email from your own domain using your own SMTP or 
transactional email service.


= Does TrustScript place any restrictions on what I can put in review 
request emails? =
Yes — and intentionally so. TrustScript enforces FTC and GDPR 
compliance rules on all email templates to protect both your business 
and your customers.

Templates are automatically checked for:

- Incentive language — words like "discount", "coupon", "gift", 
  "reward", "free", "voucher", or "cashback" are not permitted. 
  Offering rewards in exchange for reviews violates FTC guidelines.
- Rating pressure — phrases like "5-star", "highest rating", 
  "favorable review", or "best review" are blocked. Review requests 
  must not push customers toward a specific rating.
- Pressure language — words like "mandatory", "must review", 
  "required", or "have to" are not allowed.
- Review filtering — phrases like "only if you loved it" or 
  "satisfied customers only" are blocked. All customers must be 
  invited to leave feedback regardless of their experience.

Your template must also include honest-feedback language — at least 
one of the words "honest" or "feedback" must appear in the subject 
or body.

If a template violates any of these rules, it will not be saved until 
the issues are resolved. This keeps your review collection practices 
compliant, credible, and trustworthy.

= What is the "Delivered" order status? =
TrustScript adds a custom "Delivered" order status to WooCommerce. 
This ensures review requests go out only after customers actually 
receive their products — not just when the order is shipped — which 
significantly improves response rates and review quality.

= What happens if a customer gets a refund? =
If an order is marked as refunded or cancelled in WooCommerce, the 
review link expires automatically. For partial refunds on multi-
product orders, only the refunded products are removed from the review 
form — the rest of the link stays active.

= Can customers verify that a review is real? =
Yes. Every published review includes a Verified Purchase badge. 
Clicking it shows the review's unique verification token, which can 
be checked at https://nexlifylabs.com/verify-review to confirm the 
review is authentic and see a full transparency log including whether 
AI assistance was used.

= Which page builders are supported? =
TrustScript works with Elementor widgets for displaying reviews with automatic 
updates.

- Custom queue system with batch processing and rate limiting

- Each review receives a unique verification token on submission
- Public verification available at nexlifylabs.com/verify-review
- Reviews protected from editing via `wp_update_comment_data` filter
- All changes logged with timestamps
